Pizza Dough
A simple honey-sweetened pizza dough. Cook time refers to waiting while rising/waking the yeast.
PREP TIME
15 mins
COOK TIME
2 hours 10 mins
TOTAL TIME
2 hours 25 mins
Ingredients
  • 1 packet active dry yeast (2 1/4 teaspoons)
  • 3/4 cup warm water (100째F to 110째F)
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 cups flour
  • ~3 tablespoons olive oil
Instructions
  1. Using a meat thermometer, verify that the water is between 100째F and 110째F.
  2. In a medium mixing bowl, add active dry yeast, water, and honey. Mix well with a fork.
  3. Wait for 10 minutes. The mixture should now be frothy/bubbly if the yeast is working.
  4. Add flour and mix well, using your fork to press/pat the clumps together into a ball.
  5. Add a small amount of olive oil and use your fork to continue pressing the dough into a ball while spreading the olive oil around.
  6. Flip the dough ball as best as you can; it may be a bit loose. Add some olive oil and repeat the previous step on this side.
  7. Cover the mixing bowl with plastic wrap, a bag, or a lid to prevent the dough from drying out while rising.
  8. Wait 1 hour while the dough rises.
  9. Uncover the bowl and add a small amount of olive oil to coat the dough and prevent your hands from sticking.
  10. Knead the dough, and work it more into a ball shape. Return the dough to the mixing bowl and cover again.
  11. Wait 1 hour while the dough rises.
  12. The dough is now ready for use, but if preferred, you can extend or add additional rising and kneading cycles.
  13. * While the recipe suggests 1-hour waits, these can be lengthened to 2 hours if you have the time. The provided times are minimums for good results.
